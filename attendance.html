What I Learned

The Smart Attendance Check project provided me with valuable experience in implementing AI-driven solutions for real-world problems. I explored facial recognition technologies using Mediapipe and MobileFaceNet, gaining insights into preprocessing image data and achieving high accuracy in face detection and recognition. The project also improved my understanding of anti-spoofing techniques to enhance security, ensuring the system could differentiate between live faces and images. Integrating Firebase for data storage helped me appreciate the importance of cloud computing in managing attendance records efficiently. Additionally, deploying the solution on a Raspberry Pi taught me how to optimize performance on resource-constrained devices, a key skill in edge computing.
